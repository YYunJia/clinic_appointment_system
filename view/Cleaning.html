What is Scaling & Polishing?

Scaling and polishing is a professional cleaning procedure that removes plaque and tartar (calculus) that regular brushing and flossing can't eliminate. Over time, plaque hardens into tartar which can only be removed by dental professionals.

Scaling involves carefully removing these deposits from your teeth surfaces, especially from areas between teeth and along the gumline. Polishing follows scaling to smooth the tooth surfaces, making it harder for plaque to accumulate.

Regular scaling and polishing (every 6 months) is recommended to maintain healthy gums and prevent gum disease, which is a leading cause of tooth loss in adults.

Benefits of Scaling & Polishing

  • Prevents Gum Disease – Removes plaque and tartar that cause gingivitis and periodontitis
  • Brighter Smile – Removes surface stains for whiter, brighter teeth
  • Fresher Breath – Eliminates bacteria that cause bad breath
  • Overall Health – Good oral health is linked to better overall health
