Understanding Dental Braces

A tooth extraction is a dental procedure where a tooth is removed from its socket in the bone. While preserving your natural teeth is always our priority, sometimes extraction is necessary to maintain your overall oral health.

Extractions may be recommended for various reasons including severe tooth decay, advanced periodontal disease, broken teeth that cannot be repaired, problematic wisdom teeth, or to create space for orthodontic treatment.

At SmileMaker Dental, we use advanced techniques to ensure your extraction procedure is as comfortable and pain-free as possible. Our team will guide you through the entire process and provide detailed aftercare instructions.

Types of Tooth Extractions

We offer different extraction methods based on your specific needs

Simple Extraction

For visible teeth that can be removed with forceps. Performed under local anesthesia.

Surgical Extraction

For teeth that are broken, impacted, or not fully erupted. Requires a small incision.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Specialized removal of problematic wisdom teeth that may be impacted or causing issues.
