Understanding Dental Braces

Dental braces are orthodontic devices used to correct misaligned teeth and jaws. They apply continuous pressure over time to slowly move teeth into their proper positions.

At SmileMaker Dental, we offer various types of braces to suit different needs, preferences, and budgets. Whether you're looking for traditional metal braces or more discreet options, we have solutions for patients of all ages.

Our experienced orthodontists will guide you through the process, from initial consultation to final reveal of your beautiful new smile.

Types of Braces We Offer

Metal Braces

Traditional stainless steel braces that are highly effective for correcting even the most complex dental issues.

Ceramic Braces

Tooth-colored braces that blend with your natural teeth for a more discreet treatment option.

Lingual Braces

Custom-made braces attached to the back of your teeth, making them completely invisible to others.

Invisible Braces (Clear Aligners)

Virtually invisible, removable aligners that gradually straighten your teeth without metal brackets or wires.
