About SmileMaker Dental

Your trusted dental care provider in Malaysia

Why Choose Us?

Established in 2005, SmileMaker Dental has been providing exceptional dental care to the Malaysian community for over 18 years. Our team of experienced dentists and dental specialists are committed to delivering the highest standard of care using the latest technology and techniques.

We understand that visiting the dentist can be anxiety-inducing for some patients, which is why we've created a warm, welcoming environment and prioritize patient comfort throughout every procedure.

Our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art technology including digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, and advanced sterilization systems to ensure your safety and the best possible outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about dental care

How often should I visit the dentist for a check-up?

We recommend visiting every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, some patients may need to visit more frequently based on their specific oral health needs.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our administrative staff will be happy to assist you in understanding your coverage and processing your claims.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

If you experience a dental emergency such as a knocked-out tooth, severe pain, or swelling, please call us immediately at 03-19204819. We offer emergency dental services and will do our best to see you as soon as possible.

Are payment plans available for expensive treatments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for major dental procedures. Our financial coordinator can discuss various options to make your treatment more affordable.
